About us

After briefly working jobs in the big city, we (Alex and Chow) joined our parents who were running a small ice cream company. Our dad had started the company a few years earlier in the barn at home and lovingly named it after our mum, Jude. The ice cream was so good that we were convinced it should be served in the best restaurants in the country.So Alex loaded his car up with ice cream and hit the road, and Chow rolled his sleeves up and got to work in the dairy…
